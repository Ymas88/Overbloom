# overbloom

*(working title — name may change)*

overbloom is a gamified study timer that turns your focus time into a pixel-art farm. Every subject you study gets its own plot of land, and the minutes you log growing crops on that plot — the more consistently you study, the bigger and healthier your farm gets. Stop studying a subject for too long, and its crop starts to wilt, just like a real garden that's been neglected.

The goal is to make consistent studying feel rewarding in the moment, not just "good for you in theory." Instead of a plain countdown timer, you get a small farm that visibly grows because of the work you put in — and visibly suffers if you stop.

Down the line, overbloom will come with a companion Chrome extension that blocks distracting websites while a study session is running, so the timer isn't just tracking your focus but actively protecting it.

## Current status

This is very early — V1 stores everything locally in your browser (`localStorage`), with no user accounts and no backend server. The project is still being built one small piece at a time.
